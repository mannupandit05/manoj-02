Our Story

Three Decades of Sweetness & Trust

Since 1970, Sharma Sweets has been serving the city of Alwar with the finest traditional Indian mithai, crafted using recipes passed down through generations. Every sweet that leaves our kitchen is made with pure desi ghee, fresh milk and zero compromise on quality.

What began as a small family shop has grown into a beloved name — but our values remain unchanged: purity, freshness and the warmth of tradition in every bite.

  • ✔ No preservatives, made fresh daily
  • ✔ Hygienic, FSSAI certified kitchen
  • ✔ Custom mithai boxes for weddings & festivals
  • ✔ Pan-India shipping for dry sweets
